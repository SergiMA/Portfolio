Destroy The Mothership is a space combat game where there are two teams. You are in one of them and you need to destroy the mothership of the rival team. They will try to do the same thing to your team's mothership as well.

The game include mechanics such as shopping (upgrades for your spaceship or for your team), aim prediction, and complex AI behaviour where they will try to earn credits by destroying other ships and trading them to upgrade their team.

I'm excited developing this game because I'm not just trying to greatly improve my skills to create complex AI, but because I'm also trying to do everything a game requires apart from my specialisation, programming. That means I'm trying to make my own audios, modeling, VFX, etc.

Information about the game will be updated at a later date.
